Why Helga Inu is Special?

It is a decentralized crypto currency initially built on trust and loyalty on the binance smart chain. its desined with wealth distribution features. it rewards loyal holders and firmly fight against toxic actions from whales through its mechanisms.

Equity

One of the core mantra of Helga is equity. Helgainu will offer fair and equal opportunities to all members of the community irrespective of wallet size and strength.

Reward for Loyalty

Holders are the bedrock of the community and will be rewarded with 2 percent of transactions on Helgainu which is automatically redistributed to the holders pool. Holding is directly proportional to the rewards distributed to your wallet. (Y=kx)

Deflationary

50% of token supply will be burnt while the deflationary effect takes place to store and increase value. This consequently means that 2% of the token transactions will be taken out of circulation. This is a huge play for holders on a long run

Access and Anti-whale

HelgaInu is a fair community oriented token with Anti-whale mechanism to protect its market from manipulation. It is designed to give access to millions of Shiba and flokiInu lovers and traders with faint or thick pocket through its proposed bridge from BSC to ERC20.
